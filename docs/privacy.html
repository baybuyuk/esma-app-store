Gizlilik Politikasi

Yururluk tarihi: 12 Haziran 2026

Ozet: Hu uygulamasi hicbir kisisel veri toplamaz, sunucuya gondermez, ucuncu taraflarla paylasmaz. Tum veri (tesbihat sayimlari, esma secimi, ayarlar) yalnizca cihazinizda saklanir.

1. Topladigimiz Veriler

Hicbiri. Uygulama; ad, e-posta, telefon, IP adresi veya benzeri kisisel bilgi istemez, depolamaz, sunucuya gondermez. Kullanici hesabi yoktur.

2. Cihaz Izinleri ve Sensorler

Uygulama bazi cihaz ozelliklerine erisir; ancak bu verilerin tamami yalnizca cihazda islenir, disariya gonderilmez:

  • Konum (Konum servisi): Bulundugunuz yere gore namaz vakitlerini ve kible yonunu hesaplamak icin kullanilir. Konum bilgisi yalnizca cihazda hesaplanir, kaydedilmez ve hicbir sunucuya gonderilmez.
  • Pusula sensoru (Hareket): Kible yonunu gostermek icin manyetik sensor okunur. Veri kaydedilmez.
  • Bildirimler: Namaz vakti hatirlatmalari ve zikir hatirlatmalari icin yerel bildirim olusturulur. Sunucudan push bildirimi gelmez.

3. Yerel Depolama

Asagidaki bilgiler cihazinizin yerel depolama alaninda saklanir:

  • Tesbihat ve zikir sayim gecmisi
  • Esma hesabi sonucu (isminize gore hesaplanan esma)
  • Ses, yazi boyutu ve bildirim tercihleri
  • Konum tercihi (sehir)

Bu veriler hicbir sunucuya gonderilmez. Uygulamayi silerseniz bu veriler de silinir. iCloud veya Google yedekleme ayarlariniz acikssa cihaz yedeklemesine dahil olabilir.

4. Ucuncu Taraf Servisler

Uygulama; reklam SDK'si, analiz araci (Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics vb.), crash raporlama servisi veya benzeri ucuncu taraf izleme araci kullanmaz. Internet baglantisi yalnizca uygulama ilk indirildiginde (Apple App Store / Google Play tarafindan saglanan kanal) gereklidir; calismasi icin internet gerekli degildir.

5. Cocuklar

Uygulama 13 yas ve uzeri kullaniciya yoneliktir. Cocuklardan bilerek veri toplanmaz.

6. Degisiklikler

Bu politika gerektiginde guncellenebilir. Onemli degisiklikler uygulama icinden veya bu sayfada yayinlanir. Yururluk tarihi yukarida belirtilir.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

Summary: The Hu app collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and shares nothing with third parties. All data (tasbih counts, esma selection, settings) is stored only on your device.

1. Data We Collect

None. The app does not request, store, or transmit personal information such as name, email, phone number, or IP address. There is no user account.

2. Device Permissions and Sensors

The app accesses certain device features. All processing happens on-device and nothing leaves your phone:

  • Location: Used to calculate prayer times and qibla direction for your location. The location is processed on-device only, never stored, and never sent to any server.
  • Compass sensor (Motion): Used to display the qibla direction via the magnetic sensor. No data is stored.
  • Notifications: Local notifications are scheduled for prayer time and dhikr reminders. No push notifications are sent from any server.

3. Local Storage

The following information is stored on your device's local storage:

  • Tasbih and dhikr count history
  • Esma calculation result (computed from your name)
  • Sound, font-size, and notification preferences
  • Location preference (city)

This data is never sent to any server. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted. If iCloud or Google Backup is enabled on your device, it may be included in the device backup.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any advertising SDK, analytics tool (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics), crash reporting service, or similar third-party tracker. Internet is required only at first download (via Apple App Store / Google Play); the app does not require internet to function.

5. Children

The app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated as needed. Material changes will be communicated through the app or on this page. The effective date is shown above.
