# Hu — Store Listing Metinleri

Bu dosya Apple App Store ve Google Play Store gönderiminde kullanılacak tüm
metinleri içerir. Karakter sayıları her metnin yanında parantez içindedir.

Kategori kararları:
- Apple App Store: **Lifestyle** (birincil), **Reference** (ikincil)
- Google Play Store: **Lifestyle** (birincil)

Gerekçe: Hu didaktik bir öğretim uygulaması değil (Education elenir), salt
Mushaf/sözlük de değil (Books & Reference birincil olamaz). Günlük manevi
rutin aracı olduğu için her iki mağazada da **Lifestyle** doğru kovadır.
Apple'ın ikinci kategori hakkı kullanılarak Reference eklenir; böylece "namaz
vakti / 99 esma" aramalarında ek görünürlük kazanılır.

### App Name (2/30)

### Subtitle (29/30)
99 Names, Prayer Times, Tasbih

### Promotional Text (165/170)
On Friday the Munjiyah salawat comes to the front; gentle reminder before
holy nights. New: 7 spiritual surahs with audio recitation. Ad-free, free,
no tracking.

### Description (1758/4000)
Hu is a calm daily companion for Muslim spiritual practice. Big type, few
buttons, no clutter — designed so an older user can find what they need
in two seconds.

WHAT IS INSIDE
• 99 Names of Allah: meaning, virtues and a counter screen for each name.
  A personal name prescription is calculated from your own name using the
  classical abjad method.
• Prayer Times: calculated for your location. A gentle adhan notification
  at each prayer. Five times visible at a glance.
• Qibla Compass: a plain arrow using the phone's magnetic sensor. A small
  haptic tap when it locks onto the qibla.
• Tasbihat: post-prayer 33+33+33, tahlil and Ayat al-Kursi in one flow.
• Salawat Counters: eight well-known salawat, each with its own counter.
  On Friday the Salat al-Munjiyah is highlighted.
• 15 Short Surahs: readable text from al-Fatiha and juz Amma.
• 7 Spiritual Surahs: Yaseen, ar-Rahman, al-Waqi'ah, al-Mulk, al-Muzzammil,
  an-Naba, al-Inshirah — with audio recitation in listening mode.
• Dhikr Counter: a quiet finger counter with optional ambient sound
  (rain, sea, forest).
• Morning and Evening Awrad.
• Hijri/Gregorian calendar with holy night reminders.

DESIGN
Hu is built with older users in mind. Large type, high contrast, wide tap
targets. No pop-ups, no flashy animation.

PRIVACY
Hu collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, shows no ads.
Your location stays on your device and is only used to compute prayer
times.

USAGE
• Free, no ads.
• Works offline after the first location fetch.
• No account, no sign-in, no subscription.

## 4. GOOGLE PLAY STORE — EN (İkincil)

### App title (25/30)
Hu — Names, Prayer, Tasbih

### Short description (79/80)
99 Names of Allah, prayer times, qibla, tasbih and salawat. Calm. Ad-free.

### Full description (2208/4000)
Hu is a calm daily companion for Muslim spiritual practice. Big type, few
buttons, no clutter. It is designed so an older user can open the screen
and know what to do in two or three seconds.

WHAT IS INSIDE

99 Names of Allah
The meaning, virtues and counter for each name, all on one screen. You
also get a personal name prescription calculated from your own name with
the classical abjad method — the names most suited to you, with short
explanations.

Prayer Times
Computed for your location. The five prayers are visible at a glance, and
the time remaining to the next prayer is shown below. A gentle adhan
notification at each prayer time — never loud or jarring.

Qibla Compass
A plain arrow using the phone's magnetic sensor. No shaking or
calibration dances required. A small haptic tap when it locks onto the
qibla.

Tasbihat
Post-prayer 33 subhanallah, 33 alhamdulillah, 33 allahu akbar, tahlil and
Ayat al-Kursi in one flow. Each part moves to the next on its own.

Salawat Counters
Eight well-known salawat, each with its own counter. On Friday the Salat
al-Munjiyah is placed at the top of the list.

15 Short Surahs
From al-Fatiha to an-Nas, the short surahs in a large readable font.

7 Spiritual Surahs (Audio Recitation)
Yaseen, ar-Rahman, al-Waqi'ah, al-Mulk, al-Muzzammil, an-Naba and
al-Inshirah — read aloud in listening mode. The screen stays awake while
the surah plays.

Dhikr Counter
A quiet finger counter, with optional ambient sound (rain, sea, forest).

Morning and Evening Awrad
A short spiritual routine for the two ends of the day.

Hijri/Gregorian Calendar and Holy Night Reminders
A gentle reminder before each mubarak night.

DESIGN

Hu is built with older users in mind. Large type, high contrast, wide tap
targets. No pop-ups, no flashy animation, no distracting noise.

PRIVACY

Hu collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, shows no ads.
Your location stays on your device and is only used to compute prayer
times. No account, no sign-in, no subscription.

USAGE

• Completely free.
• No ads.
• Works offline. Only the first launch needs your location once.
